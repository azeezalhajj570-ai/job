• Common Detection Signals

These are general language patterns that often appear in suspicious or legitimate job posts.

Fraud Indicators
  • ✗Urgent language or pressure to apply immediately
  • ✗Requests for bank details or identity documents too early
  • ✗Advance payment or processing fees
  • ✗Unrealistic salary promises
  • ✗Vague employer information
Legitimate Indicators
  • ✓Clear job responsibilities and requirements
  • ✓Named company with verifiable details
  • ✓Standard application process
  • ✓Realistic compensation range
  • ✓Professional and consistent language
